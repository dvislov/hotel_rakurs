Accommodation

Pleasant atmosphere of each room, made by the combination of light colours and modern interior, will allow you to spend time in the hotel with pleasure whatever a purpose of visit. In each room, with any accommodation type you will find: a hairdryer, a safe, a warm bathrobe. Breakfast, wireless Internet, cable TV are also included in the room price.

Standard single room

Single room supposes accommodation for a single guest. Each room in this category has central air conditioning, a shower unit, a double bed, TV, a mini-bar, a hairdryer, a safe, a warm bathrobe and essentials. Wireless Internet access is freed available for guest.

Standard double room

Double room supposes accommodation for one or two guests. Each room in this category has central air conditioning, a shower unit, depending on the request, one double bed or twin beds, TV, a mini-bar, a hairdryer, a safe, a warm bathrobe, essentials. Wireless Internet access is free available for a guest.

Premium double room

Premium double room supposes accommodation for one or two guests. Each room in this category has central air conditioning, a dining area, a bathroom, a double bed, TV, a mini-bar, a hairdryer, a safe, a warm bathrobe, essentials. Wireless Internet access is free available for a guest. The distinctive feature of this type of room is enlarged living space, an exquisite interior and a picturesque view of the river Volga.

Room for disabled people

An enlarged living space, an ergonomic layout, safety handrails and an equipped bathroom will create maximum conveniences during your stay at the hotel. Room for disabled people supposes accommodation for one or two guests. Each room in this category has central air conditioning, a bathroom, a double bed, TV, a mini-bar, a hairdryer, a safe, a warm bathrobe, essentials. Wireless Internet access is free available for a guest.

Restaurant

The breakfast for our guests is served everyday in the dining area from 7.00 to 10.00. In the afternoon and evening at the restaurant located on the ground floor, you can plunge into the pleasant atmosphere and taste some European cuisine.

Conference

You will be pleasantly surprised by the informal atmosphere and the unique design of the conference room, located on the second floor of the complex, which seats up to 40 listeners. Easy seating arrangement and the separate demonstration zone are perfect for running conferences, seminars and presentations, during which you can use special menu of our restaurant for making a coffee break..
